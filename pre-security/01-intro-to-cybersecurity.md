# Introduction to Cyber Security

**Path:** Pre-Security | **Completed:** April 2026

## What I learned
Covered the two sides of cybersecurity and first overview: offensive (thinking like an attacker, 
finding vulnerabilities before real hackers do) and defensive (monitoring, 
responding to, and preventing attacks).

Used Gobuster to brute-force hidden directories on a fake bank site — found 
an unprotected `/bank-transfer` page with no access control. Simple but 
eye-opening: most real breaches aren't sophisticated, they're overlooked basics.

**Key takeaway:** Offensive and defensive are complementary. You need to 
understand both to be effective at either.
